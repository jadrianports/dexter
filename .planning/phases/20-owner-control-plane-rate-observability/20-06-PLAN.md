<objective>
Wire the OWNER-05 slash-command choke point and the OWNER-04 re-invite refusal into `bot.py`. A `DexterCommandTree(app_commands.CommandTree)` subclass overrides `interaction_check` to enforce block/silence for EVERY slash command at one place (no per-cog checks) — dispatching on the pure `decide_interaction_allowed` predicate (20-02) over the service reads (20-04). A block-check-first at the top of `on_guild_join` leaves a blocklisted guild the instant it re-invites.

CRITICAL mechanic (verified against installed discord.py 2.7.1, not training memory): `interaction_check` returning `False` does NOT dispatch to `bot.tree.error` — `CommandTree._call` does a bare `return` before the try/except that feeds `on_error`. The D-12 ephemeral refusal MUST be sent from INSIDE `interaction_check` before `return False`. Do NOT raise `app_commands.CheckFailure` and expect `on_app_command_error` to handle it.

Purpose: OWNER-05's "single choke point, no per-cog checks to remember" — a future command gets block/silence enforcement for free. OWNER-04's re-invite refusal makes the block re-invite-proof.
Output: `DexterCommandTree` + `tree_cls=DexterCommandTree` in `create_bot` + block-check-first in `on_guild_join`.
</objective>
