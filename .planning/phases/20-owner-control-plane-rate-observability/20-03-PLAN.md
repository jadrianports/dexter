<objective>
Ship RATE-01's observability core (D-08/D-09): tag every guild-attributable Gemini `chat()`/`generate_image()` call with its originating `guild_id` and count it in an in-memory per-session `dict[str, int]` on `GeminiService`, with a read accessor for `/guilds list`. Purpose: The data source `/guilds list` (20-07) surfaces so a live budget hog is line one. "This session" is the correct window for a kill-switch triaging a live run.
