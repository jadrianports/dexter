| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-04-06 | Tampering | `guild_queues` jsonb payload deserialized on boot | mitigate | Payload is `json.dumps` of a typed dict written by the bot itself; `Track.from_dict` reads only known keys with `.get` defaults; track URLs were validated before queue add; no `eval`/exec on payload |

| T-04-08 | Elevation/Misbehavior | smart rejoin into a channel | accept | Rejoin only fires when the previously-active channel still has a non-bot human (D-21); failure is caught and logged, never crashes boot |
