<objective>
Wire the Phase 6 pipeline together in the controller layer. Three couplings, all consuming contracts from Plans 01-03:

1. **Prefetch (PERF-01 / D-04 / D-06):** after `voice_client.play()` in `_play_track`, fire `_prefetch_next_track(guild, next_track, current_gen)` as a non-awaited `asyncio.create_task` (mirrors `_post_auto_lyrics`). It downloads the next queue entry with the generous `PREFETCH_TIMEOUT_SECONDS` budget, guards the generation counter at start AND after the download so a skip discards a stale result, and records timing into `PerfMetrics`. Scope on D-05: the optional "pre-resolve one further ahead than pre-download" is intentionally NOT separately implemented — next-1 download (D-04) plus the generation guard and the `PREFETCH_TIMEOUT_SECONDS`→stream-fallback budget already keep a slow/failed download from stalling the swap, which is the outcome D-05 exists to protect. Revisit if live use still shows gaps (consistent with the deferred next-3 buffer idea).
2. **Resolution cache (PERF-03 / D-07 / D-09):** in `play()`'s text-search branch, look up `get_resolution_cache` on the normalized query before `async_search`; on hit skip the search and resolve the cached video_id; on miss, search then write `set_resolution_cache` after the user picks (in `_queue_from_selection`). Direct-URL `/play` bypasses the cache entirely.
3. **Instrumentation surfacing (PERF-06 / D-17 / D-18):** instantiate `PerfMetrics` on the bot, record search/download/ttfa/cache-hit at the pipeline sites, update the hourly `cleanup_cache` call to the new `(pool, protected_video_ids)` signature, and surface `bot.perf_metrics.summary()` in the existing owner `/stats` embed (uncomment the stubbed fields).

Purpose: This is the plan that makes the speed gains real and observable. It must NOT introduce a stale-callback/double-play race (CLAUDE.md gotcha) — the generation guard is mandatory.
Output: modified `cogs/music.py`, `bot.py`, `utils/embeds.py`, `cogs/ops.py`; prefetch/timing tests in `tests/test_phase6_perf.py` un-xfailed.
</objective>
