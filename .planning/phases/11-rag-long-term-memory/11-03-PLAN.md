<objective>
Build the read half of the RAG loop: the embedding primitive and retrieval. Add
`GeminiService.embed()` driven by a SEPARATE `_embed_limiter` (~60 RPM, never the 15 RPM chat
budget), the pure scoring seam in `models/memory.py` (MemoryFact + rerank/recency/novelty/floor),
the scoped cosine ANN DB helper, and `MemoryService.recall()` that embeds the query, searches,
drops everything below the floor, reranks, caps to 1-3, and bumps `last_surfaced_at` on the survivors.

Purpose: MEM-02 (separate embedding quota, off the 3s path) + MEM-03 (top-k → floor → rerank →
cap 1-3, inject nothing when nothing clears). This is the deterministic TDD seam.

Output: a working `recall(user_id, guild_id, query_text) -> list[str]` that degrades to `[]` on
rate-limit or empty results, plus unit-tested pure scoring functions consuming the 11-02 constants.
</objective>
