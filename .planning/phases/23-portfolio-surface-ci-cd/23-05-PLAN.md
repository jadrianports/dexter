Purpose: D-04's hard constraint is the one to hold onto here — *the sarcasm lives in the copy, never in the structure*. Every color, size, weight, radius, and spacing value in it is declared and justified (contrast ratios are computed, not estimated; the type scale is capped at four sizes on purpose). Implement it as written. Where it says "exactly two weights" or "no box-shadows anywhere," those are constraints, not suggestions.

Output: a real-looking page skeleton with both CTAs live and the accessibility contract met.
