<planner_decisions>
Locked by the planner — do NOT re-litigate or re-ask the user:

- **PHASE 26 CHANGES NOTHING IN `bot.py`.** D-07 requires the idle/voice-empty teardown to
  disarm radio. `bot.py::idle_check` (`:958-965`) already calls `queue.clear()`, and 26-01 made
  `clear()` disarm radio. The same is true of `/stop` (`cogs/music.py:1731`), the stop button's
  `_do_stop` (`:1069`), and the reconnect-failure teardown (`:2204`). **Do not add a disarm call
  to any of those four sites** — `clear()` already covers them, and a redundant per-site call is
  exactly the per-surface-check pattern this codebase rejects (Critical Rule 19). `/radio stop`
  calls `disarm_radio()` explicitly because it must disarm WITHOUT clearing the queue.
- **`/radio` lives on `MusicCog`, not a new cog.** It needs the queue, the voice client and
  `_get_text_channel` — all already on `MusicCog`. `cogs/library.py`'s `playlist`/`jam` groups
  are class attributes on their cog; follow that shape. A new cog would need its own
  `get_queue` plumbing for zero benefit, and `/health`'s degraded check keys on MusicCog
  loading (Phase 9) — do not add a new cog to that surface.
- **The lookahead gate augments the PLAY branch; the AUTOQUEUE branch just forwards
  `radio=radio_armed`.** `decide_on_track_end` and its `TrackEndAction` enum stay BYTE-IDENTICAL
  — they are locked by `tests/test_playback_logic.py` and carry three named scars. Radio is a
  separate, additional gate consulted alongside the existing dispatch, never a new enum member
  (26-PATTERNS.md is explicit about this). On queue exhaustion with radio armed, the existing
  AUTOQUEUE branch must pass `radio=True` or the refill would hit `AUTO_QUEUE_MAX_ROUNDS` and
  post `AUTO_QUEUE_CAP_REACHED` mid-radio.
- **`/radio start` triggers its first refill through the SAME `should_refill_radio` gate**
  rather than an unconditional refill call. That makes the empty-queue case (arm -> refill ->
  `should_start_playback` starts playback) and the D-12 non-destructive case (existing queue
  deeper than the lookahead -> no immediate spend, the lookahead handles it later) one code
  path with no special "starting" case, exactly as D-12 asks.
- **`/radio start` requires the bot to already be in voice.** Radio DJs a room; it does not
  join one. Re-implementing `/play`'s join/auto-join logic is out of scope and would duplicate
  it. If `guild.voice_client` is None, respond with an error telling them to play something
  first.
- **`/radio start` and `/radio stop` both call `reset_auto_queue()`.** Start: 26-RESEARCH
  Pitfall 2's recommendation — radio always begins from a clean round counter regardless of a
  half-finished auto-queue session. Stop: radio-era `auto_queue_results` play/skip counts must
  not leak into the first post-radio auto-queue's `ignored_signal` calculation — that would fire
  a bogus "you skipped all my picks" from an hour of normal radio channel-surfing, the exact
  noise D-05 exists to prevent. `/stop` and idle-leave end the session outright, so their
  pre-existing non-reset behaviour is unchanged (not a regression, out of scope).
</planner_decisions>
