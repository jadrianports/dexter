<objective>
Extend the pure seam and its cache tier with a **required keyword-only** `AmbientSurface`, threaded
through `decide_ambient_channel` / `is_ambient_channel` / `resolve_ambient_channel`, so a surface must
name itself to fire and a per-guild toggle can silence it (D-22 / ONBOARD-04). Atomically update the
one tested consumer of the predicate — `cogs/events.py::on_message` — including closing the CONFIG-04
emoji-reaction hole (D-21) and splitting the shared `in_ambient_channel` into two independent
surface-keyed booleans (D-22). Add the pure `should_welcome_guild` decision (D-14) the lifecycle glue
in 19-03 will dispatch on.

Purpose: this is the load-bearing structural change of the phase. Making `surface` required with no
default is a breaking change to two locked-in signatures; it must land atomically with every
pytest-coupled consumer so the suite stays green at plan close. `resolve_ambient_channel`'s bot.py /
music.py call sites are deliberately deferred to 19-03 (they are untested glue — boot is intentionally
incomplete between waves 2 and 3).

Output: `AmbientSurface` + surface-keyed logic and service, a reaction-gated + surface-split
`on_message`, `should_welcome_guild`, and every mock-free / mock-based test that locks them.
</objective>
