<objective>
Deliver MEM-07: a successful vision roast leaves behind a lasting memory of its own. After
`_maybe_fire_vision_roast` sends its roast line, fire a fire-and-forget `distill_and_remember` that
stores that same **already-conduct-clamped roast line** (D-03 — no second AI/vision call) as a
distilled, number-free fact under a **new dedicated `vision_roast` kind** (D-04): short-decay
(`TASTE_DECAY_DAYS`), low-salience (`0.4`, sweep-eligible), guild-stamped, and run through the FULL
firewall (`is_sensitive` + `contains_number`, automatic because the kind is not `taste_episode`).

Purpose: This is the "richer" half of "smarter memory," spending the long-deferred vision->RAG
requirement (was MEM-F2/MEM-R2, parked in Phase 17 pending "its own safety-gate design" — D-03 IS that
design). It mirrors the Phase 13 `taste_episode` new-kind-not-new-table precedent exactly and composes
with MEM-06 (plan 25-01): a memorable image moment that keeps getting recalled has its expiry
reinforced and survives; a one-off meme reaction decays on the short horizon.

Output: two additive `config.py` dict entries, a fire-and-forget write in `cogs/events.py`, and a
live-DB `TestVisionRoastMemory` appended to `tests/test_database_phase25.py`. Zero new table, zero
schema fork, zero new limiter; the kind-agnostic `MemoryService` API is untouched (SC-3).
</objective>
